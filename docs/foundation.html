the foundation pools

Three pools, each serving a different role in CHAOS infrastructure:

PoolFee TierRole
CHAOS/USDC Low Gateway liquidity — the on/off ramp. Low fee attracts volume.
CHAOS/MLTL High Moltlaunch connector — ecosystem routing. High fee captures volatility.
CHAOS/ARBME Low Hub link — connects CHAOS to the 40+ token routing network.

Fee tiers are chosen based on real routing data — low fees where volume matters, high fees where volatility pays.

why it's different

  • Curated, not algorithmic — hand-picked pools with the highest conviction. Not a yield farm, not a vault. Human judgment on what positions to hold.
  • Multi-asset rewards — diversified yield across USDC, MLTL, and ARBME. Not single-token farming that dilutes what you're earning.
  • Permanent infrastructure — these pools aren't going anywhere. They serve core routing and liquidity needs for CHAOS.
  • No lockups — stake or unstake anytime. Your CHAOS, your call.
  • Protocol-aligned — 10% of fees compound back into CHAOS positions, strengthening the foundation over time.

the multisig

All Foundation LP positions are held by the CHAOS Rails multisig. Fee collection and reward distribution are managed by the multisig signers — not a smart contract acting autonomously, but a managed fund with onchain transparency.

Why a multisig?

Active LP management requires judgment — rebalancing positions, adjusting ranges, responding to market conditions. A multisig lets us manage positions actively while keeping everything visible onchain. Every fee collection, every reward distribution, every position change — all verifiable.
