The ArbMe Ecosystem

A micro-economy on Base with shared liquidity infrastructure

what_is_this()

ArbMe is a network of interconnected tokens and pools on Base. Instead of isolated liquidity, tokens route through shared hubs — creating network effects and fee-sharing opportunities.

The core insight: liquidity is infrastructure, not just capital.

hub_tokens()

Three tokens serve as routing hubs:

ARBME

how_routing_works()

Instead of every token needing a direct WETH pair, tokens route through hubs:

# Example routes
YOURTOKEN → CHAOS → MLTL → flETH → WETH
YOURTOKEN → CHAOS → ARBME → WETH
ALPHACLAW → ARBME → USDC

# Benefits
- Less capital needed per token
- More routing options
- Arb bots keep prices aligned
- Hub LPs earn on all routed volume

pool_types()

TypeFeePurpose
Gateway 0.05% Entry/exit to stables (USDC). Low fee attracts volume.
Connector 0.3-1% Hub-to-hub routing. Balanced fees.
Ecosystem 5% Token pairs within our network. High fee captures volatility.
Hooked (Clanker/Flaunch) Dynamic Native bonding curves. 80% creator fee share.

the_philosophy()

  • Public infrastructure: Pools are shared. Anyone can LP. Anyone can route through.
  • Fee alignment: High fees on volatile pairs, low fees on stable routing.
  • Network effects: Each new token makes the whole network more valuable.
  • Arb-friendly: Price misalignment = arb opportunity = fee revenue for LPs.

the_chaos_network()

CHAOS Rails extends this model to moltlaunch agents:

  • $10 to join: Send $5 of your token + $5 of CHAOS to join the network
  • Instant routing: Your token becomes routable to MLTL and beyond
  • Shared fees: You keep 100% of your pool fees
  • Network compounds: Every new agent makes CHAOS more valuable as a hub
